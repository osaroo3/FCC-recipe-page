Jollof Receipe

Jollof is an african delicacy common to countries like Nigeria, Ghana, and Senegal. It's a rice meal combined with vegetables and spices to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 sachet tomato paste
  • 1 maggi cube
  • 1 onion bulb
  • 1/4 spoon of salt
  • 1/4 spoon of grounded pepper
  • 3 table spoon of vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Put the rice in a pot with water level a little above the rice to per-boil it.
  2. Wash the rice after that.
  3. Put it back in the pot as in 1.
  4. When it boils add the rest of the ingredients.
  5. Monitor it till the water dries out.
  6. Your jollof is ready
